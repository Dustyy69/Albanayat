Who we are?

Established in 1408 AH in the Khamis Mushayt area, Al-Banayat Al-Arabia is a construction company specializing in general contracting, construction management, design, and construction. We transform visions into reality, working as a family with a commitment to building projects that meet high standards of quality and value.


With a proud legacy spanning 35 years, Al-Banayat Al-Arabia has grown to become a leading contracting company in the Kingdom. Our workforce supports our vision of providing valuable services, building community partnerships, and delivering important projects such as schools, hospitals, workplaces, government facilities, and social and cultural centers.


The owner of the company is Ibrahim Saad Al Sherani. He has laid down the foundation of the company with a clear vision and commitment to excellence.

What we do

Our Expertise Encompasses:


Office Buildings: Our expertise extends to the design and construction of modern and efficient office spaces that meet the evolving needs of businesses.
Government Projects: Al-Banayat Al-Arabia has a proven track record in successfully executing government projects, contributing to the development of essential infrastructure.
Educational Facilities and Schools/Universities: We are dedicated to creating conducive environments for learning by constructing educational facilities, schools, and universities that inspire growth and knowledge.
Tunnels: Our engineering prowess shines in the construction of tunnels, facilitating smooth and efficient transportation networks.
Commercial Centers: Al-Banayat Al-Arabia brings creativity and functionality to commercial spaces, contributing to vibrant and thriving commercial centers.
Villas & Residential Complexes: We take pride in crafting luxurious villas and well-planned residential complexes that prioritize comfort, aesthetics, and community living.
Water Tank Reservoirs: Our commitment to water management is evident in the construction of robust and efficient water tank reservoirs, ensuring a sustainable supply for communities.
At Al-Banayat Al-Arabia , our comprehensive construction services embody our dedication to excellence in every project we undertake. We strive to exceed expectations, delivering enduring infrastructure that stands as a testament to our commitment to quality and innovation.
