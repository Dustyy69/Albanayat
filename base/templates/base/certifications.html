Zakat, Tax, and Customs Authority

Al-Banayat Al-Arabia is proud to be certified by the Zakat, Tax, and Customs Authority of Saudi Arabia. This esteemed certification reflects our company's compliance with the tax and customs regulations set forth by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It signifies our commitment to transparency, accountability, and ethical business practices. As a certified entity, we adhere to the highest standards of financial integrity and contribute positively to the economic development of Saudi Arabia. This certification strengthens our credibility and demonstrates our dedication to upholding legal and regulatory requirements in all our operations.

Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development

Al-Banayat Al-Arabia proudly holds certification from the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development of Saudi Arabia. This certification underscores our company's commitment to fostering a supportive and inclusive workplace environment while adhering to the labor and social development regulations of the Kingdom. It signifies our dedication to promoting employee welfare, fostering skill development, and contributing to the social well-being of our workforce. With this certification, we affirm our pledge to prioritize human capital development and uphold the highest standards of ethical employment practices in all our endeavors.

Saudi Contracting Authority

Al-Banayat Al-Arabia is honored to be certified by the esteemed Saudi Contracting Authority (SCA). This certification serves as a testament to our unwavering dedication to excellence in the field of engineering and construction. Through meticulous attention to detail and adherence to the highest industry standards, we have successfully executed numerous projects across Saudi Arabia. The SCA certification not only reaffirms our reputation for delivering projects of exceptional quality but also underscores our ability to overcome complex infrastructural challenges with innovation and expertise. It is a reflection of our commitment to upholding the highest standards of professionalism and integrity in all our endeavors. Our collaboration with SCA exemplifies our relentless pursuit of excellence and our contribution to the advancement of Saudi Arabia's construction industry.

the General Authority of Zakat and Tax

Al-Banayat Al-Arabia proudly presents its Value Added Tax (VAT) Registration Certificate from the prestigious General Authority of Zakat and Tax (GAZT) of Saudi Arabia. This certification signifies our compliance with the tax regulations set forth by the Kingdom, demonstrating our commitment to transparent and ethical business practices. By obtaining this certificate, we affirm our responsibility as a corporate citizen to contribute to the economic development of Saudi Arabia while upholding the highest standards of financial integrity. Our partnership with GAZT reflects our dedication to legal compliance and our role in supporting the country's fiscal objectives. This certificate further reinforces our reputation as a trustworthy and accountable entity in the construction industry.
